*4. Cookies*

a. What are Cookies

Our website uses cookies to make our offer more user-friendly, effective, and secure. Cookies are small text files stored on the user’s computer by the user's browser. I use cookies to make the website more user-friendly. Some elements of my website make it necessary to identify the requesting browser after a page change. The following data are stored in cookies and transmitted:

* Language settings
* Log-in information

I also use cookies that enable an analysis of the user's surfing behavior. The following data can be transmitted this way:

* Search terms entered
* Frequency of page views
* Use of website functions

The data collected in this way are pseudonymized by technical precautions. This means the data can no longer be assigned to the user. The data are not stored together with other personal data. Users are informed about the use of cookies for analysis purposes via an information banner when accessing the website and are referred to this Privacy Policy. The information also includes instructions on how to prevent the storage of cookies through browser settings. The legal basis for using cookies is Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f DSGVO.

The purpose of using cookies is to simplify the website's use. For the following functions of the website, recognition of visitors through cookies is required. The collected user data is not used to create user profiles. Data are transferred to the United States.

c. Storage Duration, Objection, and Elimination Options

Cookies are stored on the user's computer and transmitted from there to our website. The user has full control over the use of cookies. In the settings of the Internet browser, the transmission of cookies can be disabled or restricted. Stored cookies can be deleted. If cookies are deleted, some parts of the website may not be fully usable.
